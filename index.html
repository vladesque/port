I'm Vlad — a multimedia producer and editor focused on social-first, branded, and real estate content. My background spans broadcast comedy at Saturday Night Live — where I worked as both Post-Production Assistant and Production Coordinator — daily news podcasting with Spotify and The Wall Street Journal, and multi-channel video production at Homes.com.


I work hands-on from planning to final cut, with a direct, collaborative approach built for fast-moving teams. AI-assisted workflows let me move faster without sacrificing the human craft that makes stories land.
