About Vlad

I am Vlad a multimedia producer who can take any of your projects from start to finish - whether it’s a corporate explainer, a journalistic podcast, a comedy sketch, or a real estate social promo. All it takes is one Vlad to do them all. How come? While earning my Bachelor’s degree in Film Production and Marketing from Brooklyn College, I was already working in production and post- production at Saturday Night Live, turning theory into practice in real time. After college, I joined Spotify, where I worked on a daily news podcast co- produced with The Wall Street Journal. I later returned to Saturday Night Live before continuing my journey at Homes.com, where I work as a multimedia producer. My role spans photo and video editing, repackaging footage for new channels, and creating promotional and educational content.
