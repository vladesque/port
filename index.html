01 — About

The story
behind the cut

I'm Vlad, a multimedia producer and editor. I've worked in post-production on Saturday Night Live, first as a Post-Production Assistant and later as a Production Coordinator. Before that I cut daily episodes of The Journal at Spotify and The Wall Street Journal, and more recently I've produced branded and editorial video at Homes.com.


I stay involved from planning through the final cut. I use AI tools where they genuinely save time, but the editing decisions stay mine.
