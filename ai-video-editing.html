Case Study

Fizz Dreamberry

Brief / Role / Tools / Timeline
Brief
Competition submission — a comedic fantasy ad for a fictional soda brand, fully AI-generated.
Role
Concept, prompt design, generation, edit, sound, delivery (solo).
Tools
Runway (video generation), ElevenLabs (VO), DaVinci Resolve (finishing).
Timeline
[TKTK]
Process

I started with the concept — a fake fantasy soda ad with a character who could carry the comedy. Before any generation, I built a reference image that locked in the character's face, outfit, and lighting so every Runway shot afterward stayed consistent. Without that anchor, the model drifts.

Generation was iterative. Each shot went through multiple prompt refinements in Runway — tightening camera moves, action beats, and lens feel until the take read cleanly. Bad generations got cut fast; the goal was a small set of usable plates, not a big library of partial wins.

Finishing happened in DaVinci Resolve. Grade to pull the palette into something warm and filmic, ElevenLabs VO timed to action beats, sound design layered under every cut. Pacing is where AI work lives or dies — the edit has to feel intentional, not assembled.
